LiquidRecO documentation

LiquidRecO is a reconstruction library for particle detectors based on the LiquidO opaque scintillator detector technology.

It provides functionality to create 3 dimensional “hits” from 2 dimensional planes of wavelength shifting fibers. And to fit particle tracks to those 3D clouds of hits.
